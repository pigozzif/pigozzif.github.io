University of Trieste


I am a PhD student in Computer Engineering at University of Trieste (Italy).

In particular, I am interested in

  • Evolutionary Computation
  • Artificial Life (especially, Evolutionary Robotics)
  • Artificial Intelligence

I am researching at the Evolutionary Robotics and Artificial Life Lab under the guidance of prof. Eric Medvet, who is partially supporting my scholarship by means of the Google Faculty Research Award 2019 he was the recipient of. The funded project is on “representations and learning algorithms for highly reconfigurable modular soft robots”.
